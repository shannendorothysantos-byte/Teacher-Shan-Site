Meet Teacher Shan 💖

Hi, I’m Shannen Dorothy T. Santos—but call me Teacher Shan! I’m a Licensed Professional Teacher, TEFL-Certified, holder of TESDA’s Contact Center Services NCII, and an ALS Training Certificate. I’ve taught kids, teens, college students, adults, and even foreign learners—so I know how to adjust and keep lessons engaging for everyone. 🌍

I graduated Magna Cum Laude, consistently landed on the Dean’s List, and proudly ranked Top 2 of my entire graduating batch. I was nominated for Best in Research at our 2023 Research Colloquium and shared my passion as a speaker in our college’s English Month podcast. Teaching is my calling—but learning is too. Right now, I’m exploring bookkeeping because words & numbers are both powerful. 📊

📚 Certified bookworm: from classics to modern lit, I devour stories like snacks.
🌱 Lifelong learner: always adding new skills (even outside teaching!).
🤝 Youth leader & barangay volunteer teacher—giving back fuels me.
🎮 Gamer at heart (Roblox, ML, COD) — yes, I can bond with students beyond lessons.
